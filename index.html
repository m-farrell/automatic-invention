Who is Honey?

Honey is an 8 year old Morkie-Mix. Born in Puerto Rico and rescued at the age of 2. Finding her way to Jenkintown, PA Honey, named Linda at the time, caught the eye of a woman named Mary on her way to work. Immediately Mary knew she had to foster this adorable doggy in the window and reach out to her Aunt Joan. After talking for a while about this mystery pup Joan and her two daugheter Maura and Grace made there way to meet "Linda".

6 Years Strong

June 24, 2014 marked the day Honey officially came home. She now runs the house and has a fair set of rules that all the humans abide by. She has gourmet meals and very comfy sweaters for the winter.
